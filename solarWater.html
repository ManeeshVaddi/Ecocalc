Solar Powered Water Heating


Solar water heating systems use solar radiation as energy to heat water for home usage. These systems are made up of solar radiation-absorbing solar collectors, which are often placed on rooftops. Warmth is transferred from the heated fluid to the water in a storage tank via a heat exchanger. A large amount of a household's hot water needs can be met by solar water heaters, which are both reasonably priced and environmentally beneficial.
